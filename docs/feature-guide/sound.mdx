## Option Menus

Rows with a triangle expose the controls listed below.

| Option | Menu control | What it changes | Attempt status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Audio Splitter | Refresh devices | Refresh FMOD output devices visible to Celeste. | Regular clean |
| Audio Splitter | Music | Choose the output device for the music audio route. | Regular clean |
| Audio Splitter | SFX | Choose the output device for the sound effects audio route. | Regular clean |
| Audio Splitter | Ambience | Choose the output device for the ambience audio route. | Regular clean |
| Audio Splitter | UI | Choose the output device for the UI audio route. | Regular clean |
| Audio Splitter | Unclassified | Choose the output device for sounds Akron cannot classify into another route. | Regular clean |
| Allow Low Volume | Music volume | Set forced low music volume. | Regular clean |
| Allow Low Volume | SFX volume | Set forced low SFX volume. | Regular clean |
| Audio Speed | Policy: Normal/SyncTimescale/Independent | Choose normal speed, Akron timescale sync, or an independent audio speed. | Regular clean |
| Audio Speed | Speed | Set the independent audio speed multiplier. | Regular clean |
| Pitch Shift | Policy: Preserve/FollowSpeed/Independent | Choose normal pitch, pitch following audio speed, or an independent pitch. | Regular clean |
| Pitch Shift | Pitch | Set the independent pitch multiplier. | Regular clean |
