StartPos is Akron's public position and room-state restore workflow. It is designed to avoid silently applying risky or mismatched restores.

## Current StartPos Shape

StartPos can capture a position, room, map SID, facing, dash count, stamina, idle/grab state, and Akron-managed restore data when Akron can safely preserve it.

Use the `StartPos` tab for public player workflows: set, load, clear, choose a slot, place with the mouse, and configure respawn-at-StartPos behavior.

## Why A StartPos May Be Blocked

Akron may block or refuse a StartPos restore when:

- The current ruleset does not allow the action.
- The saved StartPos belongs to a different map SID or room.
- The saved runtime state is unavailable or unsafe for the current map.
- The current player state cannot be restored safely.
- The action is state-changing and is blocked or marked under the active clean/submission policy state.

## Recovery Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the policy state">
    Confirm no clean-run policy state is blocking StartPos restore or other state-changing tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use a non-submission setup when intended">
    Switch setups only when the goal is room-lab routing, testing, or another Cheat workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the StartPos slot">
    Confirm the active StartPos slot is set, belongs to the current map, and was captured in the intended room.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check map policy">
    If a map is known risky, keep the safe map policy instead of forcing unsafe restore behavior.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture evidence">
    If behavior still looks wrong, record the map SID, visible policy/status state, Akron setup, StartPos slot, logs, and screenshots.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Unsafe restore overrides bypass risk blocking and are Cheat. Use them only in non-submission setups; do not use them for submitted attempts.
</Warning>

## Safe Mode Stat Guards

Safe Mode stat guards prevent selected local stat fields from being dirtied by guarded Akron sessions. Suboptions that freeze deaths, jumps, or best-run stats are Cheat because they mutate or protect tracked stats.
